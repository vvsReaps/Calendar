EDGE LAB — HELP

GETTING STARTED

Enter your TopstepX username and API key in the top bar and press CONNECT. Both fields are masked and saved locally on this device only — nothing is ever sent anywhere except directly to TopstepX. The API key comes from TopstepX Settings → API (requires a ProjectX API subscription). Once connected, live NQ and ES data streams into both charts and the ACCOUNT button can load your active Evals/XFAs.

CHARTS

NQ on the left, ES on the right. Scroll to zoom, drag to pan, double-click to snap back to live. TF buttons sync the timeframe on both charts. The 15m, 1H, 4H, and Daily charts keep the deeper archive in compact aggregated form, while raw 1-minute data is limited to the latest two weeks. The footer shows the available candle count. Hover any candle for its stats (range, body, delta) — the crosshair syncs across both charts by time. RESET VIEW restores default zoom. THEME lets you change the background and candle colors.

REPLAY & BACKTESTING

Press REPLAY to enter backtest mode. Replay keeps only a rolling 14 calendar days of raw 1-minute NQ and ES history for smoother navigation; older data is retained only as compact higher-timeframe candles. Use the 1m/1s switch to load true ProjectX one-second historical bars around the selected replay time. In 1s mode the current candle forms second by second without exposing the completed minute early; PLAY supports 1×–60× and STEP advances +1s. The wider slider remains minute-based for quickly choosing a date/time and reloads the surrounding second window after you release it. All trading, rules, and day rolls run on the replay clock. The slider locks while a position is open.

TRADING & TOPSTEP RULES

BUY/SELL work on the NQ chart (set contracts with −/+, FLAT closes). The NEXT ORDER controls let you set SL and TP in points or total dollars across the selected quantity; the converted values are shown beside the inputs. In PAPER mode, fills use simulated slippage and commissions. Use the ACCOUNT button to select an actual Topstep Eval/XFA; live-mode entries are sent as market orders with attached stop-loss and take-profit brackets. Dragging SL/TP updates the corresponding TopstepX order. CANCEL ALL cancels every working order on the selected actual account across all contracts, but it does not flatten positions.

You start on the 50K EVAL: reach $53,000 with best-day consistency at or under 50% to pass. Passing unlocks the 50K FUNDED account: balance starts at $0, the Maximum Loss Limit starts at −$2,000, trails your end-of-day balance, and locks at $0. Contracts scale 2 → 3 (at +$1,500) → 5 (at +$2,000), updating at session roll. Positions auto-flatten at 4:10 PM ET. Hitting the MLL permanently closes the account.

On the funded account, each day closing at +$150 or better is a winning day. At 5 winning days, REQUEST PAYOUT withdraws 50% of your balance and the counter resets for the next payout cycle.

INDICATORS

FVG — 3-candle fair value gaps with a 50% midline; open gaps are outlined, filled gaps fade with the box ending at the fill.
SMT — NQ×ES swing divergences. Solid lines are confirmed; pulsing "SMT LIVE" markers appear in real time the moment one market sweeps a swing the other holds (SWEPT vs HELD labels).
LIQ — external swing highs/lows as dotted lines: red while unswept (open draws on liquidity), white once swept.
LVLS — session levels: previous week high/low (green), previous day high/low (amber), Asia 20:00–00:00 ET and London 02:00–05:00 ET session ranges (dashed), and the live day high/low (dotted).
FLOW — order flow: large resting DOM walls as bars at the right edge (live only, needs Level 2 data) and oversized trade prints as bubbles on the candles (green lifted the ask, red hit the bid).
EMA 9 — 9-period exponential moving average on the current timeframe.

DOM LADDER

The DOM button opens a draggable price ladder for NQ or ES. LV1 shows best bid/ask from quotes; LV2 shows the full depth ladder (requires the CME Level 2 data add-on on your Topstep account). The VOL PRO column is the session volume profile — traded volume at each price — and works on Level 1 data. Not available in replay.

DRAWING TOOLS

Floating toolbar (draggable): rectangle with midline, horizontal line, horizontal ray, and trendline. Drawings can extend into future space to the right of price. Click a drawing to select, Delete/Backspace removes it, Escape deselects. The trash button clears all drawings from both charts (with confirmation). Shift-drag measures price and time between two points.

DATA & BACKUP

Settings, the paper account, theme, drawings, and your last account selection are stored in your browser tied to this page's location. BACKUP intentionally excludes the TopstepX username and API key. RESTORE also ignores credentials contained in older backup files. RESET in the account bar starts a fresh 50K paper Eval.

QUICK KEYS

Escape — cancel drawing tool / close popups · Delete — remove selected drawing · Double-click chart — snap to live · Shift-drag — measure.
